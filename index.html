Vision

Empowering Educational Community

Mission

To provide financial assistance to meritorious students who cannot afford to continue their higher education owing to their financial diflicultles.

Objectives

Post Finance support to students for achieving academic excellence, generate greater employability & to enhance youth involvement for creating better society

SERVICES

The three major services by Vidyaposhak Organisation are

Yakshagana

The organisation is helping the old-aged Yakshagana artists who are renowned by their Yakshagana skills in any level like bhagvathike, make up etc. As per the surveys conducted by the organisation it's analysed that they are not getting any benefits from government, private sectors. And hence are not leading the life easily. So, this organisation is helping those people who suffered all day long throughout their life to earn some benefits for entertaining people.

Scholarship

This organisation provides financial and academic support to meritorious students who are unable to continue their higher education due to economic difficulties. Post Finance support to students for achieving academic excellence, generate greater employability & to enhance youth involvement for creating better society Associating with teaching community to enhance the quality of education. It also helps to motivate people in participation at large to contribute for development of education.

Yakshagana Training

This is the new programme started recently by vidyaposhak. It involves teaching students a bit of Yakshagana on a weekly basis. The interested students can join the class for free and will be trained in it. They will be made to perform in open stage and school functions to experience. Usually the lady part in yakshagana will be handled by male artists only. But this programme involves the female candidates also. A mentor from Yakshagana background is assigned for each school.

How To Join Vidyaposhak?

Step 1: Fill Online/Offline Application

The applications can be sent by filling online application form in this portal or offline. For offline, the application form is distributed among the head masters of government schools available in the district. You can get and printed and submit the filled form via post to the following address.

Vidyaposhak

Yakshagana Kalaranga (R.) Udupi 576101.

Step 2: House Visit

Our team will be visiting to all of the applicants houses. We will be having set of questionnaire regarding the poverty level of your parents. They will be registering your responses and leaving.

Step 3: Shortlisting Genuine Candidates

In this stage we will be deciding the candidates who are joining us in this academic year. The selection is done by a panel of us. The criteria is purely based on the poverty level and academics.

Step 4: Distribute the Scholarship

One day will be decided ( usually in the month of August ) to distribute the scholarship. The scholarship amount will be some part of fee you paid to your respective colleges. The amount for Engineering students mainly depends on your CET rank. If your rank is less than 1000, you will be given scholarship amount of greater than INR 30,000 /-. You will be mapped to one particular donor.

Step 5: Rules to follow

You should write four letters to your respective donors. This will increase the amount of money that donor is donating to the organisation. Four letters can be sent in the following periods.

  1. After recieving scholarship amount.
  2. After midsem exam.
  3. After endsem exam (without waiting for results).
  4. After getting endsem results.

Step 6: Renewal the Scholarship

One can renewal for the scholarship if the previous sem/exam marks are greater than 70%. Writing four letters is a must for renewal. If you can't do this you can surrender the amount on scholarship distribution day itself. So that many other genuine users can utilise this service.

About Us

Vidyaposhak (VP), a registered society, was started in 2001 with a belief that investment in higher education provides significant dividends far beyond individual achievement to contribute for overall betterment of the society. Youth are aspiration of any country. Entire community and the nation as a whole, benefits from a greater, effective and quality higher-education. It is here that Vidyaposhak brings in interventions to handhold the student community to sail through safe and strong to achieve their academic goals on one hand and to lead a quality professional and personal life, which ultimately causes for the effective quality development of the nation as a whole. The heart of Vidyaposhak’s philosophy lies in the slogan: “empowering the student community.” Empowerment is a broad and multifaceted concept, and Vidyaposhak strives through its various programs to touch on every aspect of empowerment. Part of that is of course economic empowerment. In order to succeed in the contemporary world, a degree from an accredited institution is absolutely necessary for economic mobility, and Vidyaposhak aims to provide impoverished but academically bright students with the financial resources without which they would be unable to pursue their higher education. Without economic support higher education would be often be unthinkable for Vidyaposhak beneficiaries, and VP strives to take a holistic approach even towards financial assistance: this is why students are not simply given a cheque to cover their college fees, but are also provided with resources to cover their transportation cost and importantly to cater to need of text books, through a network of library support.
